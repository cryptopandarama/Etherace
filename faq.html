About

etherace is a brand new team-based countdown lottery game. Users are grouped into teams based on the first byte of their address. Team One: 0x0..., 0x1..., 0x2..., 0x3..., 0x4..., 0x5..., 0x6..., 0x7. Team Two: 0x8..., 0x9..., 0xa..., 0xb..., 0xc..., 0xd..., 0xe..., 0xf.

Getting Started

In order to play etherace, you need a web3 capable browser to connect to the Ethereum blockchain. We recommend MetaMask for desktop and TrustWallet for mobile. The game is played with Ether. You can acquire some from reputable exchanges like Coinbase or Shapeshift. Advanced users can directly interact with the smart contract via Remix, MyCrypto, or Etherscan.

Rules
  • The team with the highest buy volume when the clock expires wins the pot.
  • The pot is divided among the winning team members, proportional to a weighted share of team volume.
  • Each team has a different share price that starts at 1 share = 1 finney. Share price increase linearly at a rate of 0.02 finney per ETH of buy volume.
  • Every new buy adds time to the clock at the rate of 1 second/finney. The timer is capped at 24h.
  • You can also reduce the clock at the rate of 1 second/finney, but this does not count towards your share. The timer can't go below 5 minutes with this method.
  • Referrals and dividends are distributed by team. 50% of each new buy is proportionally split between that team's members.
  • New seeded rounds with new teams will begin on a semi-regular basis based on user interest. Each game will use a new contract.
  • In the unlikely event of a tie, the pot is distributed proportionally as weighted shares of total volume.
  • There is no maximum buy, but large buys receive proportionally fewer shares. For example: at the beginning of the game, one 100 ETH buy would receive 33,333 shares, whereas one hundred 1 ETH buys would receive 55,265 shares.
  • No contracts allowed.
  • Users can withdraw earned dividends from referrals or pot wins at any time. Shares cannot be sold.
  • The round will automatically open based on a preset timer.
  • The contract will be closed no sooner than 100 days after the round ends. Any unclaimed user funds left past this time may be lost.
Strategy
  • This game is designed to support multiple modes of play.
  • Get in early and promote your team to collect divs.
  • Manage risk by playing both sides of the fence.
  • Flex your whale wallet by front running and reducing the timer.
  • Piggy back on big players by making sure you're on the same team.
  • Gain a larger share of divs by supporting the underdog.
  • Buy smaller amounts to maximize your share count.
